## Daily Wallet

---

#### Budgeting that makes sense. The easiest and fastest budgeting app available.

---
Other budgeting apps make you think of your life like a business. They want you to track every penny and every category. You waste hours reconciling every minor expense with your checking account every month. This isn't how people operate. Keeping your spending under control need not be a chore.

Instead, DailyWallet just asks that you do one thing: Keep track of how many dollars you spend in your daily life. No categories, no cents, and no account reconciliation. 

Set up takes just a minute to get your Daily Spending Limit, the amount of money you can spend every day without eating into savings or bills. Every day we'll add your Daily Spending Limit into your Available wallet. Then, any time you spend money throughout any given day, take a second and record the dollar amount you spent. This money will come out of your Available wallet. Spent less than your limit? Great, the leftover money rolls over to the next day. Went a little over? That's ok too, because we'll be depositing more tomorrow.

The result is that day-to-day financial stress is completely dissolved. You know the answer to "can I afford this?" at all times. Post-purchase guilt is a thing of the past, because you know you had that much to spend.
